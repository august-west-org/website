How it works

One command. Everything running.

August West installs on any Linux machine you already own, or hardware we ship pre-configured.

01

Run the installer

One command on any Ubuntu machine — a spare computer, a mini PC, or hardware we ship pre-configured.

curl -fsSL https://augustwest.org/install.sh | bash
02

Everything installs automatically

Immich, Vaultwarden, Nextcloud, and Home Assistant — all configured, secured, and running in under 15 minutes.

03

Point your apps at home

Install the Immich and Bitwarden apps on your phone. Enter your home server address. That's it — you're off the cloud.

04

We watch it for you

Optional monitoring keeps an eye on your system. We alert you if anything needs attention — but your data never leaves your home.
